How to Use Here Am

Get Advanced Safety Tips & Interactive Videos

  • Stay Connected: Always let someone know where you are going and when you'll return. Use Here Am's live location sharing.
  • Situational Awareness: Avoid distractions. Observe people, exits, and safe spots in your environment.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, act immediately. Move to a public area, call a friend, or use the app's SOS.
  • Transport Safety: Use reputable ride services, share your ride details, and sit in the back seat.
  • Defensive Items: Carry a legal safety tool (pepper spray, whistle, or alarm), but know how and when to use it.
  • Be Smart Online: Avoid oversharing location or personal info on social media.
  • Digital Safety: Use strong passwords and keep your mobile device protected.
  • Emergency Numbers: Save local police, ambulance, and trusted contacts for one-tap access.
