How to Use "Here Am"

  1. Register/Login: Download the Here Am app or access the website. Create your account with your phone/email and set up emergency contacts.
  2. Update Your Profile: Add basic personal info, medical details, and select trusted contacts to alert in emergencies.
  3. Share Live Location: Use the "Share Live Location" feature to let friends or family track you in real time when you feel unsafe or are traveling.
  4. SOS & Emergency: In emergencies, tap the SOS button or shake your phone (if enabled) to instantly alert your contacts and share your live location.
  5. Community Alerts: Report unsafe areas, view alerts, and help keep your local community safe.
  6. Safety Tips & Videos: Access the "Guide" section for practical self-defense tips, interactive videos, and advice on staying safe.
  7. AI Chatbot: Use the AI assistant for instant safety advice, risk assessment, and quick links to helplines.
  8. Explore More Features: Try fake calls, quick routes, and more in the main menu to enhance your safety.

Remember: Stay aware, stay connected, and let "Here Am" be your companion wherever you go!
